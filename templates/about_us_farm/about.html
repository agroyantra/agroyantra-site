About AgroYantra

Nepal's dedicated AgriTech company — driving modern agriculture through equipment, technology, and expertise.

Our Mission

Modernising Nepal's Agriculture

AgroYantra (अग्रोयन्त्र) was founded with a single purpose: to bring modern agricultural technology within reach of every Nepali farmer and agri-entrepreneur.

We bridge the gap between global agri-technology and Nepal's farming needs — supplying the right equipment, building complete agri-infrastructure, and providing the expert guidance needed to make ventures profitable.

Whether you are setting up a greenhouse, a poultry farm, a fish farm, an irrigation network, or a dairy unit — we are your single point of contact from concept to commissioning.
